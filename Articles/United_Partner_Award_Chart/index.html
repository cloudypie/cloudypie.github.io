United miles have been devalued over and over again, though not at the level of SkyMiles yet. The miles are dynamic for their own flights but partners are not. What I did find is that they also follow a similar award chart style as Air Canada, a mix of zones and distance bands. However, their's is much more complicated. The prices listed below are also for non United card members. These prices are further discounted by 10% for United card holders. Layover Rules

All flights operate on the origin and destination of the itinerary. Layovers wouldn't contribute to a price increase. A flight from Lima to Sau Paulo via Panama costs 22,500 miles in economy. However, the distance from Lima to Panama is 1,458 miles and the distance from Panama to Sau Paulo is 3,154 miles for a total of 4,612 miles. If looking at the total distance flown, it should price as 35,000 United miles. If price by each segment (17,500 miles from Lima to Panama + 35,000 miles from Panama to Sau Paulo), it should price as 52,500 miles. However, the direct flight path distance from Lima to Sau Paulo is 2,160 miles, which explains why a flight from Lima to Sau Paulo via Panama costs 22,500 miles in economy.

The one exception is for Chart 3. If there is a layover for flights within Europe, then it would price as chart 1. For example an economy flight from Nice to Barcelona via Rome would price at 12,500 United miles. Meanwhile a flight from Barcelona to Rome would cost 6,000 miles and a flight from Rome to Barcelona would cost 8,000 miles.

When is United Miles Best

For those that have United credit cards, the math is much more favorable and can provide better rates against other Star Alliance programs a majority of the time. Many times, they are for the same rate and a 10% discount would put them over ahead of their competition. For direct economy flights within Europe, United offers the best pricing for Star Alliance flights. Business class flights from North America to South America and the Caribbeans that are less than 2,500 miles away will cost the lowest when booking with United miles. Furthermore, United Mileage Plus does not charge any surcharges, booking fees or change and cancellation fees that other programs often come with. For scenarios that have the same mileage cost, choose United Mileage Plus for the cheapest cash component of the award booking.
