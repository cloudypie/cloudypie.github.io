How to earn Bilt or Amex MR points when booking IHG Hotels

January 08, 2026 by Worldly Express

There has not been enough buzz around how Rakuten silently (not many creators talked about it) has been added as a transfer and earn partner for IHG One rewards. This is an impromptu article as I feel that I need to get the word out there about this deal that has gone unnoticed. The ability to earn and convert IHG points into a transferrable currency has just been created.

For context, Rakuten is more commonly known as an online shopping portal with rewards. They have the ability to convert 1% cashback into 1 Bilt or 1 Amex MR point. They are a key pillar for those in the points and miles game to quickly rack up miles. I will walk through preliminary strategies that I thought of. official page will include all the details of the rakuten and IHG partnership

Earning Rakuten on Direct IHG Bookings

The most straight forward of the bunch is to earn Rakuten points in lieu of IHG One points. You can earn 3x Rakuten points for each dollar you spend on IHG hotels. I do not recommend this approach as you are forfeiting any elite earn bonuses you would otherwise get.

Transferring IHG points to Rakuten Points

IHG now allows you to transfer 3 IHG points to 1 Rakuten point. IHG points frequently go on sale for half a penny each, meaning if you take advantage of their point sales, then this will effectively be purchasing Rakuten points for 1.5 cents per point.

Buying Points

Although buying transferrable points for 1.5 cents per point is ok, it at least sets a floor for the maximum cost we should set for obtaining transferrable points over cash back.

Welcome Offers

The real deal is converting organic IHG hotel points into transferrable currencies. For example, the current offer on the Chase IHG premier cards is 140,000 IHG points. Converting that into Rakuten or MR/Bilt points would be equivalent to 46.67k points. Sometimes, the offers for the business card extend up to 200k IHG points, which converts into 66.67k MR or Bilt points. Who said we don't have access to Bilt point welcome offers.

IHG Stays

In the same line of thinking, let's apply this to the most valuable earning rate of any hotel programs: earning points for staying in their own branded hotels. IHG stays for properties that aren't candlewood suites and staybridge suites will earn 10x points per dollar spent. The same multiplier goes for using the IHG Premier and Premier business credit cards at IHG hotels and resorts. That is 3.33x MR points and Bilt points per dollar. While not industry leading for hotel stays, they are currency leading. Amex requires you to book through their travel portals to get 5x on hotels while Bilt only gives 2x. Booking stays gets better as status goes on too. At the platinum level, it is 16 IHG points per dollar, meaning 5.33 Bilt/MR points per dollar spent. If you add the earnings of the IHG Premier cards for a total of 26 points per dollar, it comes out to 8.67 Bilt and MR points per dollar spent on booking direct with IHG hotels. That is a pretty good deal to me!
