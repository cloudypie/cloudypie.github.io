Layover Rules

1 Layover: 1 layover still prices as a direct flight would. The start and final city are the only considerations. CDG - RUH - DEL will price the same as CDG - DEL.

2 Layovers: 2 layover gets a bit tricky. If the operating airline is the same throughout, say from CDG - JED - RUH - DEL all on Saudia, then the award would price the same as direct and 1 layover. However, if the operating carrier changes, then the award would be priced as if there are 2 different awards being booked on each of the separate airlines. For example, NBO - AMS - JED - BKK, where NBO - AMS - JED is on KLM and JED - BKK
